Privacy Policy

Sudoku Shelter - Cozy Cats · Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sudoku Shelter ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We designed Sudoku Shelter to collect as little data as possible.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Sudoku Shelter does not require an account. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location. Your game progress (puzzles, coins, cat collection) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

2. Advertising

Sudoku Shelter displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device information to provide and improve advertising, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as the advertising identifier / IDFA on iOS, or the advertising ID on Android)
  • IP address and coarse location derived from it
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

On iOS, we ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before any tracking takes place. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized using cross-app tracking. Where required (e.g., in the EEA/UK), we also show a consent form powered by Google's User Messaging Platform, and you can change your choice at any time.

For details on how Google uses data, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and the Google Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases

Sudoku Shelter offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs and ad removal). All payments are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never see or store your payment details. Purchase entitlements (such as "Remove Ads") are verified through the store and saved on your device.

4. Children's Privacy

Sudoku Shelter is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertising in the app is provided by Google AdMob in accordance with its policies.

5. Data Deletion

Because your game data is stored only on your device, deleting the app removes all of it. There is no server-side account or data to delete.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
